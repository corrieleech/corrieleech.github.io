Projects and work.

A few of past and ongoing projects that keep me busy.

BookSmart

Inspired by the discussion questions that can be found at the back of many books, I used Ruby on Rails and Vue.JS to create a place for book lovers who don't have the time, network or desire to join an in-person book club but crave quality discussions about the books they read. Explore the app or watch my capstone presentation for a demo.

Birding App

Currently exploring the eBird API, with the goal of creating an app that creates a list of likely birds someone will see at a given time at any location using historic data. Follow my progress on GitHub.

Interactive Authorizer Showcases

Upon reflection, this is probably the project that sparked my eventual career change. While I conceptualized the project, led its developement and execution, and wrote messaging and content, I found working with web developers and building things from scratch was super exciting. The more I tinkered in the background to get the project over the finish line, the more I wanted to learn.
